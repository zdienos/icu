Who Are We?

We are a charcoal exporting company, established with the purpose of connecting high quality charcoal producers with potential buyers. We are deliberately formed with the aim of becoming your partner and ready to establish a long-term partnership between us and buyers. Our principle is to provide the best service and experience and to sell products at low prices that are directly distributed from our manufacturer's factories.

About Us More

Indo Charcoal Universal is a trading company that is ready to supply the needs of various types of charcoal and ready to become a business partner for your company.

Our Vision

Our Misison
